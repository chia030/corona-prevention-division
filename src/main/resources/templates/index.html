Get a PCR test

A PCR test shows if you currently have COVID-19. The virus test is performed by taking a throat swab (sample) from your throat. When you book an appointment for a test here, you book an appointment for a PCR test.

Get a vaccine

With the vaccines currently approved in Denmark, you must be vaccinated twice within the space of a few weeks to achieve the full effect of the vaccination. You must therefore book times for both injections.
